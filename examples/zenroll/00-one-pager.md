## The idea in one sentence

For **independent US restaurant owners (1–3 locations)** who **struggle to run
compliant payroll with tipped employees** (tip credits, FICA tip reporting,
multi-state rules), we offer **ZenRoll**, payroll software that **automates
tip-compliance end to end**, unlike **generic payroll tools (Gusto, ADP) that
treat tips as an afterthought**. They pay because **a single tip-compliance
mistake triggers DOL penalties and back-pay claims**. `[benchmarked]`

## Jobs To Be Done

- **Functional:** run accurate, on-time, tip-compliant payroll without a bookkeeper.
- **Emotional:** stop lying awake worrying about a wage-and-hour audit.
- **Social:** be seen by staff as the owner who always pays right and on time.
- **Today they solve it by:** Gusto/ADP + a spreadsheet + an external accountant,
  or doing it manually and hoping. `[benchmarked]`

## Why now

Tip-reporting enforcement and state tip-credit law have grown more complex and
more litigated; cloud payroll APIs (Check, Gusto Embedded) now let a small team
ship compliant payroll without becoming a money-transmitter. `[benchmarked]`

## Load-bearing assumptions

1. **Reachability/CAC:** we can acquire a paying restaurant for **< $350 CAC**
   through restaurant-association and POS-partner channels. `[guess]`
2. **Demand intensity:** **≥ 8%** of targeted owners feel tip-compliance pain
   acutely enough to switch payroll providers for it. `[guess]`

## What would make me walk away

Blended CAC > $500 with no channel under $400, **or** a head-to-head where Gusto's
existing tip features are "good enough" for >70% of interviewed owners.

## One-line market shape

~280k independent US restaurants in the tipped segment × ~$950/yr ⇒ SOM ≈ $12M
ARR if we win ~1% in 3 years. `[guess]`
