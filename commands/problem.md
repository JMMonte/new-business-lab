Understand the problem behind **$ARGUMENTS** before any solution. Use the
`problem-analyst` agent (follow `${CLAUDE_PLUGIN_ROOT}/frameworks/01-problem.md`).

If I arrived with a *solution* (I probably did), **park it** and interrogate the
problem it claims to solve as if the solution didn't exist. Work the six questions:
whose problem exactly · the pain (frequency × intensity) · what they do today
(the real competitor) · what it costs them (quantified) · why it isn't already
solved · why not just buy/use what exists. Hunt for **revealed preference** (who
already pays/hacks/switches/complains). Use `/research` to turn load-bearing
`[guess]`es into evidence.

Pick a kebab-case slug and write `./businesses/<slug>/problem-brief.md` — one page,
tagged, ending in a one-line **falsifier** (the finding that would prove the problem
isn't worth solving). Finish with the gate check: is this problem worth taking to
`/ideate`, or should it be dropped?
